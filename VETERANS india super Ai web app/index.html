About Us

Founded in 2015 by Dr. B.K. Mishra, Veterans India is a national, apolitical, people-powered patriotic movement with over 3.8 million members.

Our inclusive philosophy—"Anyone who can say Jai Hind and Vande Mataram can join Veterans India"—unites citizens across all divides. We work to foster nationalism, empower youth, support veterans and martyrs’ families, and contribute to a self-reliant and progressive Bharat.

Patriotic Events

Vijay Diwas celebrations, patriotic runs on Republic & Independence Day, uniting citizens in national pride.

Education & Collaborations
